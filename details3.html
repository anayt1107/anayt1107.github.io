Date Created: Jan 28th, 2024

I modeled this wireless charging pad to replace the existing charging pad in my Tesla Model 3, because the existing pad only supported wired charging. Tesla sells a $300 wireless charging pad as an upgrade for the car, which was my inspiration to make one for much cheaper, requiring about $5 in materials. I used a generic wireless charger from Amazon, took it apart, and mounted the coil in my custom-made pad.
