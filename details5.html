This is a 3D printed butterfly sign which I modeled from a vector image of a butterfly. I added hidden side channels for RGB lights from Amazon. I made this as a birthday gift for my sister, who used it as a wall decor piece. By modeling this, I practiced many important skills in Blender, such as using a vector to create a 3D model, modeling complex lighting in Blender, and printing an object in multiple parts.
